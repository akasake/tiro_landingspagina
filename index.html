About

Today, technology is everywhere. We cannot escape it. There have been big increases in the numbers of younger children using the web for their entertainment. This creates a space where parents find hard to reach. UnGapp helps bring parents and children together by creating a fun environment where they can learn from each other.

Product

Ungapp is an innovative game that focusses on two key aspects. Firstly, raising awareness of the usage of digital products. Secondly, Giving more insight into the digital world.

By doing this, we close the gap between two generations.
